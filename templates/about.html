About this Site

ReValues is a website that displays meters of various characteristics of characters throughout ReJane by Patricia Park. Within this semester of British Literature, we have utilized meters various times to measure our thoughts on various characters throughout the novels we have read. For example, we have metered the extent of Mr. Rochester’s byronic heroism in Jane Eyre. In ReValues, I expand on this idea within a broader scope by metering the development of a characteristic of my selected characters throughout the entire novel.

The metering done in our class notes were focused on particular points in the story - only once did we compare the meterings from another point in the story to the one done in class. Within the context of the entire novel, characters develop and their positions on the meters change; I want to express that change in my Three-Part Harmony and along the way, further develop my website design ability.

For this website, I chose 5 memorable points in the story for each character’s characteristic and I give a short reason why I rated that character at that moment as I did. The rating is out of five with one being the lowest rating and five being the highest rating. There is a slider at the bottom which you can use to slide between the various points in the story. As the slider is moved from left to right, the points in the story progress chronologically. The background image also changes to be one that is relevant to that point in the story to add more aesthetic appeal.
